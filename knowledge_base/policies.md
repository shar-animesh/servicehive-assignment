# AutoStream Company Policies

## Refund Policy

### Standard Refund Terms

AutoStream offers a **7-day money-back guarantee** on all subscription plans.

**Eligibility:**
- Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of initial purchase
- Applies to first-time subscribers only
- Account must not have excessive usage (fair use policy applies)

**After 7 Days:**
- **No refunds will be issued** after the 7-day period
- Subscriptions can be cancelled at any time to prevent future charges
- Access continues until the end of the current billing period

### How to Request a Refund

1. Contact support via email or live chat
2. Provide your account email and reason for refund
3. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
4. Refunds are issued to the original payment method

### Exceptions

Refunds may be denied if:
- Account shows signs of abuse or violation of terms
- Excessive downloads or usage detected
- Multiple refund requests from same user

## Cancellation Policy

- Cancel anytime from your account dashboard
- No cancellation fees
- Access continues until end of billing period
- Data retained for 30 days after cancellation
- Easy reactivation available

## Data & Privacy

- All uploaded content is encrypted
- Videos are automatically deleted after 90 days
- Users can manually delete content anytime
- We never share or sell user data
- GDPR and CCPA compliant

## Fair Use Policy

While Pro plan offers unlimited videos, we expect reasonable usage:
- Intended for individual creator or small team use
- Not for resale or redistribution
- Excessive usage may be flagged for review
- Enterprise solutions available for high-volume needs

## Billing

- All plans billed monthly unless annual plan selected
- Automatic renewal unless cancelled
- Payment methods: Credit card, PayPal, Apple Pay
- Invoices available in account dashboard
- Annual plans offer 20% discount
