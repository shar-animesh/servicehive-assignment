# AutoStream - Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is AutoStream?
AutoStream is an AI-powered video editing platform that automatically edits your raw footage into polished, professional videos. Perfect for content creators on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and other platforms.

### Do I need video editing experience?
Not at all! AutoStream is designed for creators of all skill levels. Our AI handles the technical aspects, so you can focus on creating content.

### What types of videos work best with AutoStream?
AutoStream works great with:
- Vlogs and talking-head videos
- Product reviews and unboxings
- Tutorials and how-to content
- Gaming highlights
- Podcast clips
- Social media content
- Marketing videos

### How long does it take to process a video?
- Basic Plan: 5-15 minutes depending on video length
- Pro Plan: 2-5 minutes with priority processing

## Technical Questions

### What video formats do you support?
We support all major formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and more. Maximum file size is 10GB per upload.

### Can I edit the AI-generated video?
Yes! After AI processing, you can make manual adjustments using our built-in editor or download and edit in your preferred software.

### What about audio quality?
Pro plan includes audio enhancement that automatically:
- Removes background noise
- Balances audio levels
- Enhances voice clarity

### Do you support vertical videos for TikTok/Reels?
Absolutely! AutoStream automatically detects and optimizes for different aspect ratios including 9:16 (vertical), 16:9 (horizontal), and 1:1 (square).
